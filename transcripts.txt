🗓️ 2026-03-27 | Email Thread: Subject: Final BRD – Identity Service Redesign
From: Priya Mehta (PM)
To: Stakeholders (Tech, Product, Security, Legal)
Timestamp: 9:00 AM

All,
Please find the final BRD attached. Summary of requirements:

FR‑01: 2FA using SMS as primary, with email fallback after configurable retries.

FR‑02: Rate‑limiting: 5 attempts per 10 minutes, with lockout and notification.

FR‑03: Device trust: users can opt to trust a device for 30 days; trusted device bypasses 2FA.

FR‑04: Backup codes: 10 one‑time codes generated at 2FA setup, stored encrypted.

FR‑05: Audit trail: log all 2FA events (attempts, successes, failures, trust actions).

FR‑06: New device login notification via email.

FR‑07: Accessibility: fallback to email if SMS carrier failure is detected.

NFR‑01: 99.9% uptime for 2FA verification service.

NFR‑02: Sub‑second latency for code verification.

Please provide sign‑off by EOD tomorrow.
– Priya
